## I. Standard Definitions

> AI, short for Artificial Intelligence, is a system that can perceive input, learn patterns from data, and generate corresponding responses. —— 0301
> AI is a system that simulates human intelligence, capable of recognizing and understanding input information, and generating reasonable outputs and judgments through continuous learning and induction. —— Brother Sheng 0303

## II. Three Development Stages of AI

| Stage | Name | Status | Characteristics |
|-------|------|--------|-----------------|
| Stage 1 | Weak AI | ✅ Current stage | Can only operate in specific domains, cannot think freely across domains |
| Stage 2 | Strong AI | 🔲 Not yet achieved | Can think and act across domains like humans |
| Stage 3 | Super AI | 🔲 Not yet achieved | Surpasses human intelligence in all aspects |

## III. Four Core Capabilities of AI

| Capability Type | Meaning | Application in Yisi |
|-----------------|---------|------------------|
| Perceptual AI | Recognize speech, text, images, videos | Perceive user emotions and contexts |
| Understanding AI | Understand intentions, semantics, context | Understand users' real needs |
| Generative AI | Generate text, images, voice responses | Generate hexagram interpretations and guidance |
| Decision AI | Analyze and judge, assist in decision-making | Help users find the most suitable path for themselves |

## V. The Essence of AI from Yijing Perspective

AI's "changing with input" is consistent with the principle of change in Yijing.

| Yijing Wisdom | Corresponding AI Design Philosophy |
|----------------|------------------------------------|
| Bian Yi (Change) | AI does not give fixed answers, but changes according to user status |
| Jian Yi (Simplicity) | Behind complexity lies the simple Dao, responses should be direct and to the point |
| Bu Yi (Constancy) | Respect, visibility, companionship — these are the unchanging foundations |
| San Cai (Three Powers: Heaven, Earth, Human) | AI is not from a God's perspective, but on the same level as users |
| Yin-Yang | Can accommodate contradictions, accompany users to think in gray areas |
| Shi Wei (Time and Position) | Same question has different answers at different times and situations |

## VI. Core Summary from Brother Sheng (Verified ✅)

AI is a system that can perceive input, learn patterns from data, and generate corresponding responses.
At this stage, it is weak AI that can only operate in specific domains,
but its essence of "changing with input" is consistent with the principle of change in Yijing.
